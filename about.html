Who Am I?

I'm Deepak Lungeli, a multi-talented individual from Kawasoti, Nepal. I'm a passionate web developer, lyrical rapper, music producer, and writer. My journey blends creativity, culture, and code. I aim to express emotion and truth through technology and words.

My Interests & Passion

I am deeply interested in historical, philosophical, and spiritual subjects. Whether it’s understanding ancient cultures or exploring futuristic AI systems, I love discovering knowledge that connects people across time and space.

Skills & Expertise

As a developer, I work mainly with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and Lua. I also produce music using FL Studio and REAPER, and write poems, stories, and thought-provoking articles for my audience on YouTube and Facebook.

My Vision

I believe in using digital tools to empower expression and accessibility. One of my goals is to build software and experiences that are inclusive for visually impaired users, while also offering emotional, creative value through writing and music.

Life Philosophy

For me, curiosity is life’s fuel. I live by the idea that “Expectation kills happiness,” and instead focus on growth, exploration, and contributing something meaningful to the world around me.
