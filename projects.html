Accessible Notepad App (Python)

I developed a notepad application using Python with a focus on screen-reader accessibility. It includes keyboard-friendly navigation, text-to-speech, and customizable font size for visually impaired users.

Music Production (x-Beat Official)

Under the name “x-Beat Official,” I produce emotional and poetic music. I use FL Studio and REAPER to compose, mix, and master tracks, including instrumental rap beats and lyrical songs. My creations are shared on YouTube and other platforms.

Romanized Nepali to Unicode Converter

Currently developing a GUI-based Python app that converts Romanized Nepali lyrics to Nepali Unicode. It also generates alternative lyrical versions while preserving the rhythm and bar length for songwriting.

Online Presence Tracker for Friends

Built a desktop app that collects updates from a friend’s social platforms (Facebook, YouTube, TikTok), displays likes, comments, and new posts in a simple interface, and exports summaries to share.

Lua Extension for Jieshuo+ Screen Reader

I have explored and contributed Lua scripts for Jieshuo+ (a screen reader for Android), to improve voice feedback and app accessibility for blind users, inspired by tools like NVDA.

Philosophical Writing & Digital Publishing
